HOTEL

Information about hotels in tourist destinations.

Risemount Premier Resort Da Nang

Located in the My Khe Beach district in Da Nang, 500 m from My An Beach, Risemount Resort Danang features two outdoor pools and barbecue. The resort has a fitness centre and spa centre, coupled with a hot tub and a sauna.

1 Night: 987.188 VND
Sea Corner Boutique Hotel & Apartment

Located in Da Nang, Sea Corner Boutique Hotel & Apartment offers beachfront accommodation 200 m from My Khe Beach and provides various facilities, such as a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. This 3-star hotel offers room service and a concierge service.

1 Night: 749.000 VND
Dreams Hotel

Dreams Hotel is located in a quiet area just 3 km from the Da Nang city centre. The hotel houses a business centre and offers complimentary Wi-Fi access.
Just steps from dining options, the hotel is 500 m from Bac My An public beach and a 10-minute drive from the Da Nang International Airport.

1 Night: 499.000 VND
Sen Boutique Hotel

Sen Boutique Hotel features a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Da Nang. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

1 Night: 650.000 VND
Gio Bien 2 Hotel

Set in Da Nang, 200 m from My Khe Beach, Gio Bien 2 Hotel offers accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. Among the facilities at this property are room service and a concierge service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

1 Night: 399.000 VND
Zalo Sea Hotel

Located in Da Nang, Zalo Sea Hotel offers beachfront accommodation 100 m from My Khe Beach and provides various facilities, such as a restaurant, a shared lounge and a garden. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

1 Night: 640.000 VND
