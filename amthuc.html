Cuisine

Information about Danang cuisine.

  • Steamed crabs

    Crab belongs to the Crab family but has a thinner body and softer shell, firm and sweet flesh. Therefore, crabs can prepare many delicious dishes. One of the easiest ways to cook crabs is steamed crabs.

  • Grilled squid

    Fresh squids that have just been picked up from the sea will be washed, seasoned and then grilled on a charcoal stove. The taste of satay squid is unmistakable with the extremely stimulating aroma.

  • Huong Snail

    Considered a relatively delicious dish among sea snail specialties, as the name suggests, this type of snail is always gently emitting a natural, attractive aroma even when they are fresh.

  • Crab with Tamarind Sauce

    If you are a seafood fanatic, you will definitely not be able to forget the strong aroma from the smell of sea crab and tamarind sauce rising up in a windy afternoon.

  • Clam

    All kinds of scallops or scallops are sweet, soft, and fragrant, grilled on hot coals with fragrant onions and peanuts that will make you unforgettable.

  • Nem Lui

    Nem lui is quite popular in many Central Central and South Central provinces. From Da Nang back, people often eat spring rolls with crispy grilled rice paper.
